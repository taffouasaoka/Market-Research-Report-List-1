<p><ul><li>Non-Isolated Driver</li><li>Isolated Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The H-bridge gate drivers market consists of two primary types: non-isolated drivers and isolated drivers. Non-isolated drivers directly control the power switching devices without electrical separation, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for low-voltage applications. In contrast, isolated drivers provide electrical separation between the control circuit and the power circuit, enhancing safety and reducing noise interference, making them suitable for high-voltage applications. Both types serve critical roles in driving motors and power converters in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
