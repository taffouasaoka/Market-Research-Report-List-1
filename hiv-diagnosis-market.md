<p><strong>The HIV Diagnosis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>Viral Load Tests</li><li>CD4 Tests</li><li>Tests for Early Infant Diagnosis</li><li>Tests for Viral Identification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HIV diagnosis market encompasses various test types, each serving unique purposes. Antibody tests detect antibodies produced in response to HIV infection, while viral load tests measure the amount of HIV in the blood, indicating disease progression. CD4 tests assess the immune system’s health by counting CD4 cells, crucial for monitoring HIV patients. Early infant diagnosis tests identify HIV in newborns, and viral identification tests help characterize specific HIV strains, aiding personalized treatment strategies. Together, these tests enhance HIV management and care.</p></p>
