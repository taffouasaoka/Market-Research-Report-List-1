<p><ul><li>Ceiling Mount Occupancy Sensors</li><li>Wall Mount Occupancy Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Passive Infra-Red (PIR) occupancy sensors market features two primary types: ceiling mount and wall mount sensors. Ceiling mount occupancy sensors are installed on ceilings to detect movement within a designated area, making them ideal for large spaces like auditoriums and warehouses. In contrast, wall mount occupancy sensors are positioned on walls and are more suitable for smaller rooms and corridors, providing focused detection in confined spaces. Both types enhance energy efficiency by automating lighting and HVAC systems based on occupancy.</p></p>
