<p><p>The performance apparel market is primarily categorized into three types: synthetic, cotton, and wool. Synthetic fabrics, like polyester and nylon, excel in moisture-wicking and durability, making them ideal for high-intensity activities. Cotton offers breathability and comfort, favored for casual and low-impact use, though it retains moisture. Wool, particularly merino, is prized for its temperature regulation, moisture management, and odor resistance, suitable for both outdoor activities and everyday wear. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance and comfort requirements.</p></p>
