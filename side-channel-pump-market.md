<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The side channel pump market is typically divided into two primary types: Type I and Type II. Type I pumps are designed for high efficiency and are commonly used in applications requiring low flow rates and high discharge pressures, such as in chemical processing. Type II pumps, on the other hand, are suited for handling larger volumes with lower discharge pressures, making them ideal for waste management and sewage applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing performance and reliability.</p></p>
