<p><p>5G Broadcast Technology encompasses various market types, primarily Standalone Broadcast and Mixed-mode Multicast. Standalone Broadcast allows dedicated transmission of multimedia content directly to end-users, enhancing coverage and quality without relying on traditional networks. In contrast, Mixed-mode Multicast combines broadcast and unicast methods, enabling efficient content delivery by simultaneously streaming to multiple users while still supporting individual connections. Both approaches leverage the low latency and high capacity of 5G networks, catering to diverse applications like live events and streaming services.</p></p>
