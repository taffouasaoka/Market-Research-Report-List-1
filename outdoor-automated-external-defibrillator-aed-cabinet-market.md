<p><p>The Outdoor Automated External Defibrillator (AED) Cabinet market primarily features two types: Wall Mounts and Semi-Recessed units. Wall Mounts are designed to be affixed directly to external walls, providing easy accessibility while ensuring protection against the elements. In contrast, Semi-Recessed cabinets offer a blend of surface and embedded installation, allowing a portion of the cabinet to be recessed into the wall for a more streamlined appearance while still offering protection and visibility for AEDs in outdoor environments.</p></p>
