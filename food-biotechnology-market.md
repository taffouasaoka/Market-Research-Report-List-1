<p><p>The food biotechnology market encompasses two key types: transgenic crops and synthetic biology-derived products. Transgenic crops are genetically modified plants that incorporate genes from other species to enhance traits like resistance to pests or improved nutritional content. On the other hand, synthetic biology-derived products involve the design and creation of new biological parts, devices, and systems, enabling the production of food ingredients and additives with enhanced properties. Both types contribute to sustainable agriculture and innovative food solutions.</p></p>
