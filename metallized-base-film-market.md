<p><ul><li>BOPP Film</li><li>PET Film</li><li>CPP Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallized base film market comprises three main types: BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film, PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film, and CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film. BOPP film is known for its excellent clarity, moisture resistance, and printable surface, making it ideal for packaging. PET film offers superior strength, dimensional stability, and barrier properties, commonly used in food packaging and electronics. CPP film is characterized by flexibility and heat resistance, making it suitable for flexible packaging applications. Each type serves distinct market needs in packaging solutions.</p></p>
