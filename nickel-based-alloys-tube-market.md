<p><ul><li>Seamless Tube</li><li>Welded Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel-based alloys tube market is divided into two main types: seamless tubes and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured by extruding a solid block of metal, resulting in a strong, consistent material without joints. These tubes are ideal for high-pressure applications. Welded tubes, on the other hand, are created by rolling and welding flat metal strips, making them more cost-effective for certain applications. Both types are widely used in industries such as aerospace, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p></p>
