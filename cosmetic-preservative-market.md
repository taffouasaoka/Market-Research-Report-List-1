<p><p>The cosmetic preservative market includes several key types. Paraben esters inhibit microbial growth and maintain product integrity. Formaldehyde donors release low levels of formaldehyde for preservation. Phenol derivatives offer effective antimicrobial properties. Alcohols, such as ethanol, serve as both preservatives and solvents. Quaternary compounds, known for their antibacterial properties, enhance product stability. Lastly, organic acids, like sorbic acid, prevent mold and yeast growth. Each type plays a vital role in extending shelf life and ensuring product safety.</p></p>
