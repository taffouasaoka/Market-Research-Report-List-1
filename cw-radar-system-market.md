<p><p>The CW radar system market is categorized into various frequency bands: L Band, X Band, C Band, and S Band, each serving distinct applications. L Band, with lower frequencies, is ideal for long-range surveillance and tracking. X Band offers high-resolution imaging, suitable for weather monitoring and marine applications. C Band strikes a balance between range and resolution, often used in civilian air traffic control. S Band is effective for medium-range detection, commonly found in radar for aviation and missile systems.</p></p>
