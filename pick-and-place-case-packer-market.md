Semi-automatic packers blend human involvement with machinery, enhancing speed and consistency while still needing some manual input. In contrast, automatic packers fully automate the packing process, providing high throughput, reduced labor costs, and minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale production environments.</p></p>
