<p><p>The flying jetpack market can be categorized by the type of propulsion used: gas-based and liquid-based systems. Gas jetpacks utilize compressed gases, such as helium or hydrogen, to create thrust, allowing for lighter designs and maneuverability. In contrast, liquid jetpacks rely on combustible fuels or specialty liquids, offering higher thrust and longer flight durations but often requiring more complex engineering. Each type presents unique advantages and challenges, catering to different consumer needs, applications, and regulatory considerations.</p></p>
