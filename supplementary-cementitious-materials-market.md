Ferrous slag, produced during metal smelting, contributes to improved mechanical performance and resistance to chemical attack. Both materials are increasingly adopted in construction to promote eco-friendly practices and reduce reliance on conventional Portland cement, supporting sustainable infrastructure development.</p></p>
