<p><p>The transmission jacks market consists of two main types: stand-alone and adapter types. Stand-alone transmission jacks are independent units designed to lift and support vehicles' transmissions during repairs or replacements. They offer stability and ease of use. In contrast, adapter types are attachments that enhance the versatility of existing jacks, allowing them to accommodate various transmission sizes and shapes. Each type serves unique mechanics' needs, influencing their selection based on workshop requirements, space, and specific tasks.</p></p>
