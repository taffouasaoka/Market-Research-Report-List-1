<p><strong>The Industrial Girth Gear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spur Girth Gear</li><li>Helical Girth Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial girth gear market comprises primarily two types: spur girth gear and helical girth gear. Spur girth gears feature straight teeth and align parallel to the gear axis, making them efficient for lower speed applications but producing higher noise levels. In contrast, helical girth gears have angled teeth that allow for smoother engagement and quieter operation, making them suitable for higher speed and heavy-load applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including cement, mining, and power generation.</p></p>
