<p><p>Food grade lubricants and greases are categorized into three types: H1, H2, and 3H. H1 lubricants are formulated for incidental food contact and are safe if ingested in small amounts. H2 lubricants are designed for areas where food does not come into contact but are safe to use in food processing environments. 3H lubricants are specifically used in food applications and are suitable for direct food contact. These classifications ensure compliance with safety regulations in food production and processing.</p></p>
