<p><p>The Non-Mechanical Surface Treatment Equipment market includes technologies like plasma treatment and corona treatment, both used to enhance surface properties of materials. Plasma treatment involves exposing surfaces to ionized gases to improve adhesion, wettability, and cleanliness, making it ideal for applications such as coatings and printing. Corona treatment utilizes high-voltage electrical discharges to modify surfaces, increasing their surface energy and promoting adhesion for inks and adhesives. Both methods are crucial for improving material performance in various industries.</p></p>
