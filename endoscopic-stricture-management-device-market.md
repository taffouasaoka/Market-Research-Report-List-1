<p><p>The Endoscopic Stricture Management Device market encompasses various types, including balloon dilators, stents, and bougie dilators. Balloon dilators are inflatable devices used to widen narrowed passages in the gastrointestinal tract. Stents are tubular mesh-like devices that help keep strictures open over time. Bougie dilators are solid, tapered instruments designed to gradually stretch strictures. Together, these devices are essential for treating strictures in various organs, aiming to restore normal function and alleviate patient discomfort.</p></p>
