<p><p>Network converters facilitate the integration of different cable types in communication systems. The Copper to Fiber market focuses on converting electrical signals from copper cables to optical signals for enhanced distance and speed. The Fiber to Fiber market involves converting between different types of fiber optic cables, optimizing compatibility and performance. The Copper to Copper market emphasizes signal regeneration or management within copper connections, ensuring efficient network operations. Each type addresses specific connectivity needs in the evolving telecommunications landscape.</p></p>
