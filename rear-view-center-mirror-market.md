<p><p>The rear view center mirror market can be categorized into two primary types: electronic screen mirrors and glass screen mirrors. Electronic screen mirrors incorporate advanced technology, providing real-time video feeds from rear-mounted cameras, enhancing visibility and reducing blind spots. These mirrors often feature additional functionalities such as navigation displays and driver assistance alerts. In contrast, glass screen mirrors are traditional reflective mirrors that offer basic functionality, typically providing a straightforward view of the rear without digital enhancements. Both types cater to distinct consumer preferences and vehicle designs.</p></p>
