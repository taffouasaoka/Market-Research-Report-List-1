<p><p>A Metal Oxide Disc Varistor (MOV) is a voltage-dependent resistor that provides overvoltage protection for electronic circuits. It is composed of metal oxide materials and is designed to conduct electricity when the voltage exceeds a specific threshold, effectively clamping the voltage and protecting sensitive components from damage due to voltage spikes, such as those caused by lightning or other electrical surges.</p><p>The Metal Oxide Disc Varistor Market is anticipated to experience significant growth due to the increasing demand for electronic devices and the rising awareness of safety measures against voltage fluctuations.
