<p><p>The robotic milking machines market comprises two primary types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic milking machines. Semi-automatic milking machines require some manual intervention from operators for tasks like attaching and detaching the milking units, whereas fully-automatic systems operate independently, managing the entire milking process without human assistance. Both types enhance efficiency and animal welfare, but fully-automatic machines offer greater convenience and labor savings, making them increasingly popular among dairy farmers seeking to maximize productivity and streamline operations.</p></p>
