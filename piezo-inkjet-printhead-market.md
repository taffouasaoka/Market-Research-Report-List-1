<p><p>The piezo inkjet printhead market is segmented into two primary types: MEMS inkjet printheads and non-MEMS inkjet printheads. MEMS inkjet printheads leverage Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems technology, offering high precision and miniaturization, which enhances print quality and reduces manufacturing costs. In contrast, non-MEMS inkjet printheads utilize traditional piezoelectric materials for ink ejection, resulting in reliable performance in various printing applications. Each type serves different sectors, including commercial, industrial, and consumer printing, catering to specific needs based on performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
