<p><strong>The Rack Mounted Load Bank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Load Bank</li><li>Reactive Load Bank</li><li>Resistive/Reactive Load Bank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rack-mounted load bank market consists of three main types: resistive load banks, reactive load banks, and resistive/reactive load banks. Resistive load banks primarily simulate real power loads to test generators and UPS systems under steady-state conditions. Reactive load banks introduce reactive power to assess equipment performance in dynamics. Resistive/reactive load banks combine both functionalities, enabling comprehensive testing across various scenarios. This diversity allows for tailored applications in industries such as data centers, power generation, and equipment testing facilities.</p></p>
