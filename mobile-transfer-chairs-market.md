<p><p>The mobile transfer chairs market consists of two main types: manual and electric mobile transfer chairs. Manual mobile transfer chairs are designed for user-operated mobility, relying on human effort to maneuver and lift patients. They are typically lightweight and easily portable. Electric mobile transfer chairs, on the other hand, utilize battery-powered mechanisms to assist in patient transfer, offering enhanced ease of use and reduced physical strain on caregivers. Both types are essential for improving patient handling in healthcare settings.</p></p>
