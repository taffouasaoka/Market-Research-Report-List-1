<p><p>The Lithium Battery Management System (BMS) market encompasses two primary types: centralized and distributed BMS. Centralized BMS integrates all battery cell monitoring and management functions into a single unit, simplifying design and reducing costs, often suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, distributed BMS decentralizes these functions across multiple units, enhancing scalability and reliability, making it ideal for large battery systems like electric vehicles and renewable energy storage. Each type caters to distinct applications and performance requirements in battery management.</p></p>
