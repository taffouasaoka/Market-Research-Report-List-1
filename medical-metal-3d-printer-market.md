<p><p>The medical metal 3D printer market encompasses various technologies, including Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), which uses melted filament to create structures layer by layer. Fused Granular Fabrication (FGF) involves feeding metal granules into a print head for similar layering. Laser Metal Fusion (LMF) utilizes lasers to melt metal powder, achieving high precision and density. Other methods may include binder jetting and directed energy deposition. Each technology offers unique advantages, catering to diverse applications in medical device manufacturing and personalized healthcare solutions.</p></p>
