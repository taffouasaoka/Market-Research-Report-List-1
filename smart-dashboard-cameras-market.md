<p><p>The smart dashboard cameras market includes three primary types: Single Lens (Single Channel), Multi Lens (Dual Channel), and Rearview Dashboard Cameras. Single lens cameras capture footage from a single perspective, providing a basic view of the road ahead. Multi lens cameras use two lenses to record front and rear views, enhancing safety and situational awareness. Rearview dashboard cameras are designed specifically to provide a clear view of the rear area while driving, adding an extra layer of security for reversing and parking maneuvers.</p></p>
