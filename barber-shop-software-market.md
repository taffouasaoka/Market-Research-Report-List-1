<p><p>Barber shop software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and web-based. On-premise software is installed directly on the barber shop's local computers and offers complete control over the system, including data security, but requires regular maintenance and updates. In contrast, web-based software operates in the cloud, allowing easy access from any device with internet connectivity. This type usually involves subscription pricing and automatic updates, offering convenience and minimizing IT responsibilities for the barber shop owners.</p></p>
