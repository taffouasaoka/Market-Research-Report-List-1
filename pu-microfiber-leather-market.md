<p><p>The PU microfiber leather market comprises two primary types: solvent-based and water-based PU microfiber leather. Solvent PU microfiber leather is produced using organic solvents, providing durability and a premium feel, making it suitable for high-end applications like luxury fashion and automotive interiors. In contrast, water-based PU microfiber leather utilizes water as the primary solvent, resulting in a more environmentally friendly product with lower VOC emissions, appealing to eco-conscious consumers and industries targeting sustainable practices. Both types serve diverse markets, balancing performance and environmental impact.</p></p>
