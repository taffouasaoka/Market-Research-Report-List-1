<p><p>The full-size mobile C-arms market is categorized into two main types: those with pulsed fluoroscopy and those with continuous fluoroscopy. C-arms with pulsed fluoroscopy deliver intermittent radiation, reducing patient exposure while providing high-quality imaging, making them suitable for procedures requiring precision. In contrast, continuous fluoroscopy offers a continuous stream of radiation, which allows for real-time imaging, beneficial in longer procedures. Both types are essential in surgical, orthopedic, and diagnostic applications, catering to varying clinical needs.</p></p>
