<p><strong>The Anti-Fog Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Foggy Guard Coating (FGC)</li><li>Defog Coating (DFC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-fog coatings market comprises various types designed to mitigate fogging on surfaces. Foggy Guard Coating (FGC) creates a hydrophilic layer that minimizes fog by spreading water into a thin film, enhancing visibility. In contrast, Defog Coating (DFC) employs a different approach, typically using a hydrophobic treatment to repel water droplets, thereby preventing fog formation. Both coatings are widely utilized in applications such as eyewear, automotive, and industrial settings, improving safety and comfort by maintaining clear visibility.</p></p>
