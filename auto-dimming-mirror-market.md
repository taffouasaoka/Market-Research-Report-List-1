<p><strong>The Auto-Dimming Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Electrodynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto-dimming mirror market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and electrodynamic. Mechanical auto-dimming mirrors utilize a system of plates or mechanical components to adjust the mirror's tint based on light exposure. In contrast, electrodynamic mirrors employ a liquid crystal or electrochromic technology that alters light transmission through electrical control. Both types enhance driving safety by reducing glare from headlights, but electrodynamic mirrors offer smoother, more responsive dimming capabilities.</p></p>
