<p><p>The oral local anesthetics market comprises various agents used for pain management during dental procedures. Lidocaine is the most commonly used anesthetic due to its rapid onset and effectiveness. Mephocaine offers a reduction in toxicity and is often used in specific patient populations. Prilocaine is known for its low systemic toxicity and is suitable for longer procedures. Articaine is favored for its ability to penetrate bone and provide profound anesthesia. Other anesthetics include formulations like Benzocaine and Tetracaine, each serving niche applications.</p></p>
