<p><p>Wrap-around cartoning machines are classified into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention for certain tasks, such as loading products or adjusting parameters, making them suitable for small to medium production runs. In contrast, automatic machines operate independently, minimizing human involvement by automatically folding and sealing cartons, which enhances efficiency in large-scale production environments. The choice between the two types typically depends on production volume, budget, and the desired level of operational efficiency.</p></p>
